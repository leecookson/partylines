
Elements

F: FISCAL
S: SOCIAL
D: DIPLOMACY
T: TECH
L: LAW


Degrees

1 - Very Conservative
2 - Lean Conservative
3 - Lean Liberal
4 - Vary Liberal


Matching

0 is exact match
1 means an element differs by 1 degree
2 for a single elements means they differ by 2 degrees
2 for a pair of elements means they collectively differ by 2 degrees
    It could be each element differs by 1 degree
    or one element matches and the other differs by 2 degrees
